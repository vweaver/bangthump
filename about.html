About the Bang–Thump Calculator

The Bang–Thump Distance Calculator is a tool that helps shooters estimate the distance to their target using the time delay between a rifle's report ("bang") and the sound of the bullet impact ("thump").

How It Works

When you fire a rifle:

  1. The bullet travels to the target at supersonic speed
  2. The sound of the impact travels back to you at the speed of sound
  3. The total time between shot and hearing the impact can be used to calculate the distance

Using the Calculator

  1. Select a preset caliber or enter your own ballistic data
  2. Measure the time between shot and impact sound (in seconds)
  3. Click "Calculate Distance" to get the estimated range

Custom Presets

You can save your own load data as presets:

  • Enter your ballistic data
  • Type a name for your preset
  • Click "Save Current Values"

Your presets are stored locally in your browser and will be available the next time you visit.
