About the Bang–Thump Calculator

The Bang–Thump Distance Calculator is a tool that helps shooters estimate the distance to their target using the time delay between a rifle's report ("bang") and the sound of the bullet impact ("thump").

How It Works

The calculator uses the time difference between when you hear the shot and when you hear the impact to estimate the distance. It accounts for:

  • The bullet's flight time, using a linear interpolation of velocity
  • The time it takes for the sound of the impact to travel back to you

Your custom presets are stored locally in your browser for future use.
