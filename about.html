About the Bang–Thump Calculator

The Bang–Thump Distance Calculator estimates the distance to the target using the time delay between a rifle's report ("bang") and the sound of the bullet impact ("thump").

The calculator numerically solves for the distance by modeling bullet travel time (with a simple linear velocity drop) plus the time for the sound to travel back to the shooter.

Your custom presets are stored locally in your browser for future use.
